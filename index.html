The Third Amendment: Are You Still Letting It Cockblock Your Civic Duty?

Forged in a bygone era, the Third Amendment prevents our nation's most dedicated protectors from integrating into the very communities they defend. This isn't just a constitutional relic; it's a barrier to intimacy. It denies citizens the opportunity for profound, hands-on support and prevents our service members from enjoying the warmth and comfort of a modern American home. We believe in replacing this outdated restriction with a new culture of radical hospitality.
